About

Waste is defined as unwanted and unusable materials and is regarded as a substance which is of no use. Waste that we see in our surroundings is also known as garbage. Garbage is mainly considered as a solid waste that includes wastes from our houses (domestic waste), wastes from schools, offices, etc (municipal wastes) and wastes from industries and factories (industrial wastes). There are various methods for disposing garbage however people are oblivious to them and this causes more harm to the environment as different kinds of waste products are not treated properly.

This website aims to provide knowledge as to what can be done when a particular waste product is encountered so that it can be disposed correctly.
